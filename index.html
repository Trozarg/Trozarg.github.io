About Us

Online bookseller, selling used vintage books of all genres. I tend to look for old sci-fi but I grab any book of all genres.
With a huge collection of First Printings and First Editions.
If I don't have the book let me know what it is, I bet I can find it for you.

Shipping

I try to ship out, the day of order. If I can, but please allow up to 3 days of handling time.
However if you have concerns or questions, please message me it's no bother at all.

Payment

Orders will be processed through either Ebay.
Most forms of payment are excepted, if you are in the local East Freedom, PA area and, prefer cash method please message prior to purchase.

Returns & Refund

Returns are accepted if the item/s is/are either not as described, damaged, or lost in shipping. Please however if you do request a to return item or refund message me.
